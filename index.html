📄 Research Paper
Nov 2024 – May 2025

Design, Analysis and Fabrication of 5.6 GHz E-Shaped Microstrip Antenna for ITS Applications

🏫 Associated with Panimalar Institute of Technology · Final Year Project

Designed and fabricated a compact E-shaped microstrip antenna operating at 5.6 GHz for Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS), targeting Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) and Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I) communication to enhance road safety.

  • Used CST Studio Suite and FR-4 substrate to design a low-cost, compact antenna with optimised geometry.
  • Achieved excellent performance — low return loss, wide bandwidth, and stable gain across the operating frequency.
  • Validated hardware using a Vector Network Analyser (VNA); measured results closely matched simulation data.
  • Antenna supports V2V and V2I communication protocols for intelligent and connected transportation systems.
